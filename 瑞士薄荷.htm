瑞士薄荷. 胡椒薄荷(歐薄荷)  薄荷薄荷 M. canadensis
Swiss Mint makes a fabulous tea, or use in middle eastern dishes. 
It is also great is a rockery provided you are happy for it to spread.  
Otherwise plant in a pot to contain its growth. 
It is a strong peppermint variety with pale pink flowers.
